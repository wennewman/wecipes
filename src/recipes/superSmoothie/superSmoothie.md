###Ingredients
3 cups spinach  
1 bunch kale  
2 small carrots  
1 tomato  
1 avocado  
1 banana  
1 cup blueberries  
½ cup linseeds  
½ cup pumpkin seeds  
½ red pepper  
1tbsp honey (optional)  
1 litre of cold water  
1 cup ice (optional)
##Method

1. Blend all ingredients flat out until smooth
2. Feel your belly smile as you drink it
